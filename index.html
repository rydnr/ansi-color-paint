ANSI Color Text Formatter

This tool allows you to select text in a grid, choose an ANSI color, and generate text with the correct escape sequences applied. Ideal for creating colorful ASCII art and formatted text outputs.

To generate ASCII art from text, take a look at Parorjk's Text to ASCII Art Generator.
